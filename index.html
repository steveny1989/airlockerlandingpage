安全从不妥协

Security never comes second

AirLocker 不把任何原始文档发出你的电脑：不上传云端、不调用外部识别 API，一切解析、识别、脱敏都在本地完成，满足保密、合规和审计的要求。

AirLocker never sends raw documents off your machine: no cloud uploads, no external recognition APIs—parsing, detection, and anonymization all run locally to meet confidentiality, compliance, and audit needs.

纯本地处理 On-device only 所有文档解析、识别和脱敏都在你的设备上完成，不经过任何外部服务器。 All parsing, detection, and anonymization run on your machine—never on external servers.
无外部依赖 No external APIs 不依赖第三方识别服务，无额外数据流出路径，减少供应链风险。 No third-party recognition services or extra data egress paths, reducing supply-chain risk.
可审计可还原 Auditable and reversible 生成受控的映射表，只在内部需要时还原原文，支持事后审计和责任追踪。 Controlled mapping tables let you restore originals internally when needed, with full traceability for audits.
